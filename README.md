# 🎓 Better School Choices — Mobile App Design

**End-to-End UX/UI Design Case Study**
Designed by [Somyr Woods-Mercado](https://www.linkedin.com/in/somyrwoodsmercado) · Product Designer & UX Researcher

---

## Overview

**Better School Choices (BSC)** is a mobile application designed to give parents and guardians a single, centralized hub for navigating school choice decisions for their children. Rather than jumping between district websites, review platforms, and social groups, families can compare schools, read verified reviews, and connect with the BSC community — all in one place.

This repository contains the complete end-to-end mobile interface design, from initial research through final high-fidelity screens, prototypes, and developer-ready handoff assets.

---

## The Problem

Choosing the right school is one of the most significant decisions a family makes — yet the process is fragmented, overwhelming, and inequitable. Parents are forced to:

- Cross-reference multiple websites with inconsistent or outdated information
- Rely on informal word-of-mouth with no way to verify credibility
- Navigate bureaucratic enrollment processes with little guidance
- Make high-stakes decisions without a clear, comparable view of their options

**BSC bridges that gap** by consolidating school discovery, comparison, community reviews, and direct support into a single mobile-first experience.

---

## Goals

- Design an intuitive, accessible mobile interface for parents and guardians at all tech comfort levels
- Create a streamlined onboarding flow that gets users to value quickly
- Surface school information in a digestible, comparable format
- Build trust through verified reviews and transparent data
- Enable direct communication between families and the BSC support team

## Key Screens

- **Landing / Onboarding** — Hero messaging, image carousel, sign-up and sign-in CTAs
- **School Discovery** — Search and browse by location, school type, grade level, and program
- **School Detail Pages** — At-a-glance info, photos, programs, ratings, and enrollment details
- **Verified Reviews** — Community-sourced feedback with credibility indicators
- **Chat / Support** — Direct messaging with the BSC team for personalized guidance
- **User Profile & Saved Schools** — Bookmarks, comparison tools, and account management

## Design Decisions

### 🟡 Yellow as a Trust Signal
The soft yellow (`#F3E9A0`) border treatment on the hero section was intentionally chosen to evoke warmth and optimism — emotions parents should feel when making this decision. It creates visual hierarchy without anxiety.

### 📸 Carousel-First Hero
Rather than a static illustration, the image carousel leads with real classrooms and real kids. Authenticity matters in a space where trust is the product.

### 🔵 Blue CTA Hierarchy
The blue (`#6B9CD1`) Sign In button paired with a white-outlined Create Account button creates a clear visual hierarchy — returning users can move fast while new users are still invited in.

### 🧭 Bottom Navigation Anchored to Action
Reviews and Chat are surfaced as primary bottom nav items because they represent the two highest-value, highest-trust actions a new user takes. Discovery happens through trust, not just search.

## Design System

**Typography:** Albert Sans (Bold for headings, Regular for body)
**Primary Color:** `#6B9CD1` — calm, trustworthy blue
**Accent:** `#F3E9A0` — warm yellow
**Text:** `#313131` — near-black for legibility
**Border Radius:** 20px (buttons), 12px (image cards)
**Font Scale:** 24px / 18px / 16px
